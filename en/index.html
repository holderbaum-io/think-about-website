Think About! is a 2-Day Community Event about Technology, Design and their impact on Society.

9 & 10th of May 2019, Cologne

Our Conference

Think About! creates a safe and inspirational space for everyone regardless of their personal background/preferences.

We want to bring together diverse individuals and make them challenge themselves.

Our Topics

  1. Think About
    Tech!

    We will present topics that touch all areas of technology such as: Microservices, Agile Software Development, Continuous Delivery & Integration, AWS, this and that and many more than that.

  2. Think About
    Design!

    We will present topics that touch all areas of technology such as: Product Design, Innovation, User Experience Design (User Research, Interaction Design), Graphic Design, Typography, Exhibition Design, ...

  3. Think About
    Society!

    We will present topics that touch all areas of technology such as: Rassmism, Discrimination, Gender inquality, Artifical Intellegence, Psychology, Economic Justice, Social Justice, and many more ...
