Think About! is a 2-Day Community Event about Technology, Design and their impact on Society.

9 & 10th of May 2019, Cologne

Our Conference

Think About! creates a safe and inspirational space for everyone, and we really mean everyone.

We want to bring together diverse individuals and make them challenge themselves and each other.

Our Topics

  1. Think About
    Tech!

    Technology has become a part of nearly every aspect of human life. We want to be a present quality talks from experts of the fields. We will cover things like Software Development, Hardware and Software Hacking, Internet of Things and Artificial Intelligence, ...

  2. Think About
    Design!

    TODO We will present topics that touch all areas of technology such as: Product Design, Innovation, User Experience Design (User Research, Interaction Design), Graphic Design, Typography, Exhibition Design, ...

  3. Think About
    Society!

    Technology and Design continues to shape our Society as a whole. Topics like Discrimination, Gender Inquality, Economic Injustice and Politics are more important than ever and we want to create a platform to talk and discuss those.

Our Location

Cinenova, Cologne-Ehrenfeld

The Conference will take place in a cozy cinema right in the center of the Ehrenfeld District in Cologne. It provides us with two big halls for talks, unlimited drinks and popcorn and of course regional organic catering services.

But do not worry about not seeing the sunlight: The cinenova has a cozy outdoor area as well.
