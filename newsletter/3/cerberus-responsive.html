Special Newsletter

February 2019

We have amazing news: Our speakers' line-up is complete!

Look forward to 24 talks from leading experts and newcomers from the tech industry with companies such as Mozilla, Travis CI, ThoughtWorks, Capgemini, Pivotal, and many more.

The speakers will present a broad range of topics from front- and backend technologies via infrarstructure through to IOT and security. Furthermore you can expect a critical discourse regarding the question “What impact do technology and design have on our society?”.

And don't forget: Our Early Bird Sale will end on February, 15th.
