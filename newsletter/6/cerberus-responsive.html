Our Solidarity Tickets Concept

We are able to offer free or reduced solidarity tickets for the Think About! Conference. Those important tickets support the overall diversity of our event as people from “outside the tech industry bubble” get access to the conference as well.

Offering a basic stock of solidarity tickets is a distinct position in our budget. Additionally every supporter ticket we sell enables us to offer even more solidarity tickets.

If you can't afford a ticket yourself please reach out to us!.
Get in touch
 

Our amazing event location: The Cinenova

We are excited to host the Think About! in the lovely arthouse cinema Cinenova in the popular district 'Ehrenfeld' in Cologne. The cinema is cozy and at the same time provides amazing acoustics and a great atmosphere for talks.

Who wouldn’t want to sit in a puffy cinema seat, eating popcorn (yes - we have a popcorn-flat-rate) and watching amazing human beings talking about interesting topics?
