Did you ever wonder who is behind the Think About! Conference?

We realised that we never really introduced ourselves to you - so it is time to change that:

Hey to all you wonderful people out there. We are Andreas, Chrissi, Jakob and Rudi from Cologne, Germany. We are three human-beings and one dog partly with technical, partly with activist backgrounds who are realizing a long time dream to make our own conference possible. We are currently planning the Think About! conference in our free time among our daily jobs in the tech industry.

If you have any questions or feedback for us, or you simply want to get to know us please feel free to reach out at any time.
