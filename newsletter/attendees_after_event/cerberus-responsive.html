The venue: Cinenova

We have rented the beautiful Cinenova in Cologne Ehrenfeld for two days.

How to get there via public transportation: You can either take the U-Bahn 3, 4 or 13 at exit at "Venloerstraße Gürtel" or you can take the S-Bahn 12 and 19 and exit at "Bahnhof Ehrenfeld". From both stations it is only a 5-7 minute walk to the cinema.

Parking: There are different options for parking nearby. The cinenova has a pretty good overview on their website already. Check it out here

Address: Herbrandstr. 11, 50825 Köln
